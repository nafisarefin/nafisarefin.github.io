CFD-Based Embolism Modeling

This project focuses on the use of computational fluid dynamics (CFD) and Lagrangian particle tracking (LPT) to model the transport of emboli throughout the cardiovascular system.

Using patient-specific vascular geometries—including the Circle of Willis, thoracic aorta, and abdominal aorta—this study simulates blood flow and particle trajectories to assess risks of embolism-related complications such as stroke, acute kidney injury, and hepatic embolism.

Future work will involve validating these simulations using in vitro flow experiments and integrating emboli trajectories with clinical imaging data.
