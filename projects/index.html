Research Projects

Validated Emboli Tracking Study in Patient-Specific Aorta
CFD-Lagrangian particle tracking study in patient-specific vasculature to assess stroke risks.
Hemodynamics in Abdominal Aorta to assess Acute Kidney and Liver Injury
First study of its kind to track emboli and assess visceral organ injury risk during cardiopulmonary bypass.
Influence of Cannula Orientation and Atherosclerotic Plaque Disruption
Computational study to assess stroke and plaque distuption risk in patient-specific aortas of patient from different age groups with varying cannula orientation.
Emboli Transport in Aortic-CoW Pathways During CPB>
ntegrated experimental and computational study of hemodynamics and emboli transport in the thoracic aorta and Circle of Willis during cardiopulmonary bypass.
Thrombosis Risk in Aneurysmal Arteries via PIV-PLIF
Experimental study combining PIV and PLIF to investigate flow patterns and thrombus formation in aneurysmal arteries.
Aerodynamic Coefficient Prediction for Cirrus SR22
CCFD-based analysis using open-source tools and advanced turbulence modeling to predict aerodynamic coefficients for the Cirrus SR22 aircraft.
