About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, specializing in cardiovascular biomechanics, CFD, and Lagrangian Particle Tracking (LPT). My research focuses on patient-specific simulations of emboli transport during cardiopulmonary bypass, with a particular emphasis on stroke risk, cannula orientation, and atherosclerotic plaque disruption across age groups.

Research Interests

  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)
  • Fluid–Structure Interaction (FSI)
  • Emboli transport and stroke prediction
  • Patient-specific aortic simulations
  • Cardiopulmonary bypass modeling
