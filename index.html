Nafis Arefin

Hello! I am a final year PhD candidate in Biomedical Engineering at the University of Tennessee.

My research focuses on emboli transport and hemodynamics across the cardiovascular system, from the brain to the abdominal organs (Circle of Willis, thoracic & abdominal aorta) to evaluate risks of stroke, cardiac injury, acute kidney injury, and hepatic dysfunction. My background spans computational modeling (CFD, FSI, ML, CT/MRI image reconstruction) and experimental methods including PIV & medical device testing.

Before joining UTK, I received my M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from UTS, Australia, and B.S. in Aerospace Engineering from IIU, Malaysia. I grew up in Dhaka, Bangladesh.
