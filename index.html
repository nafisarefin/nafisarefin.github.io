Nafis Arefin

PhD candidate | Biomedical Engineering | University of Tennessee Knoxville



Hello! This is Nafis, working as a GRA at the Cardiovascular Biomechanics Lab at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville.

My research focus on tracking emboli throughout the cardiovascular system — from the brain to the abdominal organs, including the Circle of Willis, thoracic aorta, and abdominal aorta. Through these simulations, I study hemodynamic patterns and assess disease risks such as stroke, acute kidney injury, and hepatic complications.

I have a combined background in computational modeling — including CFD, FSI, LPT, and image-based modeling using CT/MRI — and experimental techniques such as blood pump trials and flow validation using PIV.

Before joining UTK, I completed my M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the UTS, Australia, and my B.S. in Aerospace Engineering from the IIU, Malaysia. I grew up in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

Thank you for visiting my webpage!
